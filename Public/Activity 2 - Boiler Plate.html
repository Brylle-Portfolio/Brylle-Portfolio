BALDELOVAR's BUTTERED CHICKS RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless chicken (breast or thighs)
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garammasala
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prep Chicken
    • Cut 1 lb boneless checken into bite sized pieces. Marinate with 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp garam masala, salt, and pepper.
  2. Saute Aromatics
    • In a pan, melt 1/4 cup butter. add 2 cloves minced garlic and 1 tbsp ginger paste. Saute until fragrant.
  3. Cook Chicken
    • Add marinated chicken. cook until it turns brown (5-6 mins).
  4. Finish
    • Simmer on low heat until chicken's tender (10-15mins). Adjust seasoning.
  5. Garnish
    • Top with fresh cilantro. Serve with rice.
